The Cavern Awareness Quiz

Fill out the quiz to see how mentally aware you are!


1. You had a long and tiring day, and are now ready to destress, but realize that you have to get up early the next morning. Do you… Finish up some work that isn’t top priority but you can get done early
Go to bed and get as much sleep as you can
Play some video games and stay up late until you can’t keep your eyes open

2. A research project that you had a month to work on is due the day after tomorrow. However, you were only able to complete 75 % of it due to your teammates not doing their share of work. Do you… Take some time to calm yourself and destress, before explaining to your teammates that they need to pick up their slack so you can all get the best outcome possible?
Stay up today and tomorrow night to quietly complete your work yourself?
Yell at your teammates and leave the rest of the work to them?

3. Almost all of your friends are going to try some new hallucinogenic substance that is becoming popular on the streets tonight. They insist that you have some fun and join. Will you… Reluctantly accept their invitation?
Placate them by saying that you will come a little later before not showing up?
Stand your ground and explain to them why you do not want to participate?

4. A friendly neighbor you know has confessed to sometimes not getting enough sleep because they are kept up by panic attacks. A lot of the other neighbors joke that he/she should “go to a mental asylum.” Will you... Go along with the crowd?
Publicly shame the other neighbors for their insensitivity on social media?
Discourage the negative behavior towards your neighbor and help them in any way possible?

5. A friend of yours admits that they are very stressed with all of the work piling up on them. They also think that they are developing some sort of anxiety because of it. How do you respond? "Just get over it already..."
"Oh no, it sounds like you should go see your doctor/therapist to talk about this. And remember, you can always ask me or your family members for help if you're feeling overwhelmed."
"Don't worry about it, you'll feel better in no time!"

6. Your sibling seems to have a mental condition. Do you… Make sure they know they can always come to you for help, and if needed, ask about any extremely concerning issues?
Confront them bluntly in a private space?
Wait to ask in front of your parents during dinner?

7. You feel a little empty without any productive work to do and nothing on your priority list. Do you… Passively scroll through your phone in boredom even though you know you aren’t paying much attention to it?
Spend your time hanging out with friends and engaging in fun outdoor activities?
Pull an all-nighter at a party with alcohol to waste your time?
