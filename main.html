Stress, at times, can be proven to be beneficial- leading to improved immune systems and motivation but more so than often, there are negative effects. Long-term stress can cause issues such as anxiety or depression to arise. Many times, the stress stems from an inability to prioritize urgent and non-urgent tasks, along with a lack of time management and organization of tasks. The Mindful Matrix helps with these issues by replacing an average to-do list using the Eisenhower Method. The Matrix, instead categorizes tasks through priority and urgency, thus giving a user a sense of organization and order, so they do not feel like they have scattered priorities. Its easy-to-use functions and visibly pleasing outlook provide a better state of mind for the user and help the user destress, organize their thoughts, and keep track of everything they need to accomplish.

Stress is just another part of life we accept - one that 73% of people admit affects their mental health.
